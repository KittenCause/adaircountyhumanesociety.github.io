About Us

Our mission is to provide a nurturing and safe environment for the homeless, abandoned and neglected animals in the Adair County area.

We are a privately run 501 (c)(3) non-profit organization. Donations are welcome and tax-deductable.

Adoption Fees

  • Adult Cats: $115 and up
  • Kittens: $40 ($40 sterilization deposit)
  • Adult Dogs: $120 and up
  • Puppies: $40 ($40 sterilization deposit)
The sterilization deposit is paid upon adoption and returned once proof of sterilization is presented.

Volunteering

We happily accept offers to volunteer at the shelter. All volunteers must attend an orientation. The calendar below shows the dates of upcoming orientations, as well as other important dates at the shelter.

Volunteering hours are 12 PM - 4 PM on weekdays.
For more information, give us a call at (660) 665-8038
